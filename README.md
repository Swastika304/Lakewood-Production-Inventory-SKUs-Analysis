# Lakewood-Production-Inventory-SKUs-Analysis
Power BI project analyzing Lakewood plant production and inventory performance, including revenue, cost, SKUs, defects, and supply chain efficiency.

# Key Insights & Features:
1.Total Revenue and cost analysis 
2.Financial and sales performance by Plant
3.Top SKUs by Inventory Cost & Revenue
4.Cost Distribution & Defect Rate by Product Category
5.Average Delivery Delay by Year
6.Top Vendor by Stock Distribution
7.Delivery Details Table
8.Interactive filters for Year and SKU

# Feedback & Collaboration:
Collaborated with stakeholders to define key performance indicators (KPIs), ensure data accuracy, and deliver an interactive dashboard that supports inventory planning and informed business decision-making.
